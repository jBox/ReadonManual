Getting Books

Readon now contains 3 ways to get your books, including getting from Online Resources, from SkyDrive and open from browser directly. Here are the first approach, a simple and quick way to get books from Online Resources, the other two approaches will introduce in later chapters.

Tap the Online Resources Icon Button

Go to location tab, tap the Online Resources icon button to enter the Online Resources, you will complete the task witch is to get books from Online Resources.

Go Online Resources window, tap thebutton, a dialog box pops up named Resource URL, you can enter your URL in the text box: such as http://epub-samples.googlecode.com/files/epub30-spec-20121128.epub, then tap the check button or press the Enter key, the dialog box disappears, a record will be added into Online Resources downloads list.After completed, this record will disappear, press (physical Back key) will back to the Home, you can see the book displays in the recent.

Opening Book

In the recent reading list, tap the book cover. When you first open a book, it will take seconds to initialize the book.

Book Initialization

After that finished, it brings you to the reading window, now, you are free to read it, enjoy reading!

Reading

Introduction

Reading Window

You can flicking at Reading Window, flicking left or right, book will be turning pages up and down. Flicking up will navigate you to Operator board, and down will get the Contents of book. Reading Windows included Book Title, Text, Chapter Title, Index/Pages, Bookmark Tag and Notes Tag.

Tips: Only at the Reading Window, tapping the Back key (physical key) will exit reading.

Contents (EPUB only)

Flicking down at the Reading Window will open CONTENTS. when CONTENTS opened, you can tap the Back button or tap Up button to turn off it.

Tap Arrow Button

You can click on the arrow button to enter subdirectory (if it contains subdirectories), sub-window, the top is the parent directory, you can click the button to return to the parent directory, you can also slide to the right to return the parent directory. Click the name of the directory, enter the reading window and navigate to the linked page.

Flick Right Back to Parent

Bookmarks

Open the Control Panel in the state, click the bookmark k button to add bookmarks, bookmark button will light, and then click the button, a bookmark button to return to the original state, the bookmark is deleted. In the information panel on the move to the bookmark tab, you can view all bookmarks, click Bookmarks records, you can navigate to the page that contains the bookmark.

Bookmarks

Notes

Notes and bookmarks are very similar, the difference is, notes can record information on each page you can add a sticky note. On the control panel click on the Edit button to open the notes, add content, add notes, click the Delete button, you can delete notes. Control panel you can see the current page notes, notes can slide up and down to see the entire contents. Notes content is not more than 500 characters.

Book Information (EPUB only)

In the information panel, view book information, including pictures, titles and so on.

SkyDrive

Connect to SkyDrive

If you want to download books from SkyDrive, then Readon need to connect to your SkyDrive.

Open SkyDrive

In the main interface, click "Open SkyDrive" button, if not connected to SkyDrive, you will authorize pages, Readon need to connect to your SkyDrive, view basic information, offline access, and e-mail address, etc. privileges. Authorized, you can "read" the function to browse your SkyDrive SkyDrive directories and files.

Getting books from SkyDrive

If Readon has been linked to SkyDrive, you can see the directory structure in SkyDrive, click on the directory will be listed within the directory structure, through the Readon of SkyDrive feature, you can view and download. Txt and. Epub files.

SkyDrive control bar

Note: Click on the "up" button to return to the parent directory, click the back button (physical return key) to exit SkyDrive

Downloads

In SkyDrive control bar, click on the "download" button to open the download list, you can view or manage the file being downloaded and download failed files.

In fact, online resources include not only books, but also a resource web page, you can try in the address bar enter http://wwww.google.com, see what happens?

Reading Stats

Interface Description

Read the statistical records your daily reading time, in accordance with the minute statistics (less than one minute by one minute statistics). Reading time is started from statistics, the total time you read. Icon Description daily reading time statistics, the vertical axis represents the reading time (minutes), the abscissa indicates statistical date (day).

Read the statistics interface

Remaining is the rest of your reading time (if you do not purchase a license), if the remaining time is less than or equal to zero, you will not be able to continue to read books, you can read the time of purchase, you can also purchase a license.

License

Purchase a license

Each Readon initially contains 360 minutes of reading time, and if you read the remaining time is less than or equal to zero, you will not be able to continue reading.

Open Reading Stats, tap the link to Get more reading time, open store, purchase a license, you will get unlimited reading time.
